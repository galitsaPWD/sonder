what is sonder?

Sonder is the realization that every passerby has a life as full and complex as your own — filled with late-night thoughts, quiet heartbreaks, small joys, unfinished sentences.

why this exists

This project is a gentle place for that realization to live. An emotional atlas where strangers leave poems, memories, songs, and moments on the places that shaped them. Nothing here asks for your name. Only what the world has felt like from where you are standing.

the creator

Writer · developer · observer of people. Building small, quiet tools for noticing.
